Week 3: Detection Techniques (Chromatography)

Chromatography is frequently used by chemists to purify and/or separate liquids
(and solids.) An impure sample is loaded onto a column of adsorbant (Stationary
Phase), such as silica gel or alumina or resin. An organic solvent or a mixture
of solvents (the eluent) or mobile phased moves down through the column.
